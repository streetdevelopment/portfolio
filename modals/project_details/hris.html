Project Summary

Developed during my internship at Pixzel Digital, the Human Resource Information System (HRIS) was designed as a comprehensive solution to streamline HR processes for organizations of varying sizes. The goal was to enhance efficiency and accuracy in managing employee information, attendance, payroll, and reporting. The system features secure organization registration and login, an intuitive dashboard, and modules for company setup, employee profiling, attendance tracking, leave and overtime management, and payroll processing. It simplifies tasks such as photo-verified clock-ins, leave requests, and automated payroll calculations while providing robust reporting and analytics to enhance decision-making. This project highlighted my ability to design user-friendly interfaces, integrate complex backend systems, and deliver scalable solutions for organizational efficiency.
